    About me




    Illustrator and
    2D Gamer Artist



    Brenda Failache is a Illustrator and 2D Game Artist. Worked in many projects as Art Director and creating art for educational games muiltplaform, with Augmented Reality and so on. Illustrations for books, comics and animations, in national and international projects. Begin working as colorist for Comics in 2009, has been the winner by Archive at the time for commercial projects, then changed to illustration works since then and begin to work as 2D Game Artist. Been working into projects that brings educational and environmental consciousness with creativity. Worked as Art Director, Illustrator and 2D Game Artist for projects made by Mentor Tecnology, 3D Productions, Maria Studio, Bitx Software House, and Ludus Studio. Also was member on collectives such as Collective Argonautas, M.A.R Mulheres Artistas, and GameDevsPa, in this one was Art Judge. Made presence in some Game Jams as Global Game Jam.
    Hobbies
    Playing RPGS, JRPGS, games that brings some peacefulness, and cozy feelings, puzzles and escapism, but also adventure. Watching animes, reading mangas (joseis, slice of life), books and comics in genres like sci-fi, horror and fantasy. Also listening to different genres of music, like pop, metal, and jazz.
